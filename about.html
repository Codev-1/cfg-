Gearless Traction Machine

A gearless traction machine is an elevator drive system where the motor shaft is directly connected to the traction sheave (no gearbox). This allows smooth, quiet, and energy-efficient elevator operation. A proven partner to elevator manufacturers across India.

Machine Encoders

A machine encoder is a precision feedback device mounted on the elevator motor (or traction sheave) that continuously reports. This data is sent to the elevator controller / VVVF drive, allowing smooth, safe, and accurate elevator travel. Machines, doors, brackets & complete lift solutions.

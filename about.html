Precision Elevator Components

High-performance machines engineered for reliability. Elevator suitable for all types of constructions for catering to the need of vertical transport of Passenger and Goods, customised from concept to delivery in different types lifting options like Traction, MRL and Hydraulic Elevator System.

Gearless Traction Machine

A gearless traction machine is an elevator drive system where the motor shaft is directly connected to the traction sheave (no gearbox). This allows smooth, quiet and energy-efficient elevator operation. A proven partner to elevator manufacturers across India.

Elevator Doors

Elevator doors are precision-engineered safety components designed to ensure smooth opening and closing operations at every floor. Equipped with advanced sensors and interlocking mechanisms, they provide passenger safety, noise-free movement and reliable performance. Available in automatic and manual configurations, our elevator doors are built for durability, efficiency and seamless integration with modern lift systems including Traction Machines, Hydraulics & complete lift solutions.

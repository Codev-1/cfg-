Traction Machines

CFG Components supplies high-performance geared and gearless traction machines engineered for smooth operation, durability, and long-term reliability in residential and commercial elevators.

Geared Traction Machine

Our geared traction machines are built with rugged mild steel construction and precision worm gear mechanisms, making them ideal for mid-rise residential and commercial elevators.

Material: MS
Usage: Elevator
Surface Finish: Paint Coated
Frequency: 50 Hz
  • Rugged MS Construction for heavy-duty use
  • Geared Mechanism ensures controlled speed
  • Cost-Effective with easy maintenance

Gearless Traction Machine

CFG gearless traction machines use direct-drive technology to deliver ultra-smooth, silent, and energy-efficient elevator performance, ideal for high-rise applications.

Voltage: 240 V
Material: MS
Phase: Single Phase
Frequency: 50 Hz
  • Gearless Technology for silent operation
  • Energy Efficient & space-saving design
  • Low Maintenance with long service life

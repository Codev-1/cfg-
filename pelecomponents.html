Elevator Components

CFG Components supplies a complete range of precision-engineered elevator safety, mechanical, and installation components designed to ensure reliability, durability, and long-term performance.

Over Speed Governor

A critical elevator safety device that detects excessive speed and activates the safety gear to stop the elevator car immediately.

Material: Mild Steel
Voltage: 220 V
Accuracy: +/- 0.05 m/s
Standard: EN81-20
  • Reliable Speed Detection for emergency protection
  • Durable Construction for long service life
  • Automatic reset and manual test features

Guide Clip

Guide clips securely fasten guide rails to the shaft wall, ensuring alignment and vibration control for smooth car travel.

Type: Forged/Pressed
Surface: Zinc Plated
  • High tensile steel construction
  • Vibration dampening rubber inserts available
  • Easy vertical and horizontal adjustment

Rope Clip (U-Bolt Clamp)

High-security fastening solution for terminating wire ropes. Engineered to provide maximum grip without damaging the rope strands.

Material: Forged Carbon Steel
Finish: Hot Dip Galvanized
Standard: DIN 1142 / EN 13411-5
Size Range: 6mm to 20mm
  • Heavy-duty forged base for superior stability
  • Precision threaded U-bolt for consistent torque
  • Rust-resistant coating for long-term shaft use

Elevator Guide Rail Brackets

Structural support components that anchor guide rails to the elevator shaft walls. Designed for rigid alignment and load distribution.

Material: Mild Steel (Heavy Gauge)
Type: Adjustable / Fixed
Application: T-Rail Mounting
Finish: Primer/Zinc Coated
  • Slotted holes for precise horizontal adjustment
  • High vibration dampening properties
  • Supports Cabin and Counterweight rails

Wedge Anchor Fasteners

Reliable heavy-duty mechanical anchors used for fixing brackets and machinery into solid concrete shaft walls.

Material: Grade 8.8 Steel
Coating: Zinc / Yellow Passivated
Installation: Pre-drilled Hole
Expansion: Torque Controlled
  • Deep expansion sleeve for high pull-out resistance
  • Fire-resistant and seismically approved
  • Available in M10, M12, and M16 sizes

PVC Wiring Truff (Casing)

Organized cable management system for elevator wiring. Protects electrical lines from dust, moisture, and mechanical wear.

Material: Flame Retardant PVC
Standard: UL94 V-0 Rated
Length: 2 Meters (Standard)
Features: Snap-fit cover
  • Non-conductive material for electrical safety
  • Smooth internal finish for easy wire pulling
  • Resistant to chemicals and high shaft temperatures

Limit Switch

Ensures safe elevator operation by controlling travel limits and preventing over-travel into the pit or overhead.

Contact: 1NO + 1NC
Protection: IP65 Waterproof
  • Precision snap-action mechanism
  • Cast aluminum housing for durability
  • Certified for safety circuit integration

Wire Rope

High-strength multi-strand wire ropes designed for high traction, low stretch, and safe elevator car suspension.

Diameter: 8mm - 16mm
Core: Fibre / Steel Core
  • Superior fatigue resistance
  • Optimized for traction sheave longevity
  • Anti-rust lubrication treatment

General Elevator Hardware

A complete inventory of essential washers, nuts, bolts, and specialized fasteners required for elevator assembly and maintenance.

Items: Bolts, Nuts, Spring Washers
Grade: 4.8 / 8.8 / 10.9
Packing: Bulk / Kit-based
